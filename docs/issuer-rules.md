
# Issuer Application Rules

Comprehensive issuer-specific rules governing approval likelihood and bonus eligibility. For bureau pull preferences and inquiry management, see [[application-timing]]. For tactics like credit freezes and authorized user strategies that interact with these rules, see [[advanced-techniques]].

These rules change -- verify against the source links if evaluating more than 3 months after April 2026.

## Chase

| Rule | Detail | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| **5/24** | Denied if 5+ personal credit accounts opened (any issuer) in 24 months. Applies to ALL Chase cards. | [WEB] TPG, DoC, OMAAT |
| **2/30** | Max 2 personal cards OR 1 business card per rolling 30 days | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Business card exemption** | Chase, Amex, Citi, Barclays, BofA biz cards do NOT count toward 5/24 (they don't report to personal credit). Capital One, Discover, TD Bank biz cards DO count. | [WEB] TPG, Freq. Miler |
| **Sapphire eligibility (2026)** | 48-month rule ELIMINATED. Replaced by proprietary pop-up eligibility check. Now one bonus per specific Sapphire card per lifetime (CSR and CSP tracked independently). Pop-up tells you before hard pull. | [WEB] DoC, OMAAT, TPG |
| **General bonus rule** | Most non-Sapphire cards: 24-month rule. Can't hold card or have received bonus in 24 months. Southwest family: no bonus on ANY Southwest card in 24 months. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Credit reallocation** | No max card count, but limits total credit extension. Can shift credit lines between existing Chase cards to open new ones. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Recon lines** | Personal: 1-888-245-0625. Business: 1-800-453-9719 | [WEB] DoC |

## American Express

| Rule | Detail | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Once-per-lifetime** | Cannot receive welcome bonus on same card more than once. Based on card ownership, not bonus receipt. Closing does NOT reset. Anecdotal: eligibility may return ~7 years after cancellation. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **5 credit card limit** | Max 5 Amex credit cards. Does NOT include charge cards (Platinum, Gold, Biz Plat, Biz Gold). | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **10 charge card limit** | Max 10 Pay Over Time (charge) cards. Personal + business both count toward respective limits. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **1/5 rule** | Max 1 new credit card per 5 days | [WEB] Freq. Miler |
| **2/90 rule** | Max 2 new credit cards per 90-day rolling window. Charge cards counted separately. OK to apply for 2 on same day. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Pop-up jail** | Amex may deny bonus (but still approve card) based on history of opening/closing cards and prior bonus receipt. Pop-up appears during application. No reliable way to escape except spending more on existing Amex cards over time. | [WEB] OMAAT, Upgraded Points |
| **Clawback risk** | Amex can reclaim welcome bonus if account closed/downgraded within 12 months of opening. | [WEB] TPG |
| **Recon** | No dedicated recon line. Call 1-800-567-1083 (status/cardholder line). | [WEB] DoC |

## Citi

| Rule | Detail | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| **48-month rule** | Cannot receive bonus on same card if bonus received in past 48 months. Counts from bonus posting date, not approval date. Each card tracked independently. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **1/8 rule** | Max 1 card application every 8 days (rolling). Personal + business combined. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **2/65 rule** | Max 2 card applications every 65 days (rolling). Personal + business combined. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **6/6 rule** | May deny if 6+ hard inquiries in past 6 months on the pulled bureau. Not a firm rule -- "a generalization" per DoC, with many data points of approvals above 6. Can request reconsideration on a different bureau. | [WEB] DoC 2026 |
| **Recon** | Written recon more effective than phone. Executive Review Dept, PO Box 6000, Sioux Falls SD 57117. | [WEB] DoC |

## Capital One

| Rule | Detail | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| **48-month rule** | Cannot receive bonus on exact same card if received in past 48 months. Different cards are eligible. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **~2 personal card limit** | Rumored max of 2 personal credit cards. Inconsistently enforced -- data points vary. | [WEB] OMAAT (flagged as inconsistent) |
| **~1 card per 6 months** | Rumored velocity limit. Inconsistent data points -- not a hard rule. | [WEB] OMAAT (flagged as inconsistent) |
| **3-bureau pull** | Generally pulls from all 3 credit bureaus (Experian, TransUnion, Equifax) on every application. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Business cards count toward 5/24** | Capital One biz cards report to personal credit. They DO add to Chase 5/24 count. | [WEB] TPG, Freq. Miler |

## Barclays

| Rule | Detail | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| **6/24 rule** | May deny if 6+ new accounts in 24 months. Inconsistently applied -- weaker than Chase 5/24. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Inquiry sensitivity** | Moderately inquiry-sensitive. May evaluate spending on existing Barclays cards. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Bonus eligibility** | Generally eligible if closed same card 6+ months prior. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Same-day consolidation** | May combine hard pulls for multiple same-day applications. | [WEB] OMAAT |

## US Bank

| Rule | Detail | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| **Very inquiry-sensitive** | Among the most inquiry-sensitive issuers. Existing banking relationship strongly recommended. | [WEB] DoC |
| **Altitude Reserve relationship req** | Altitude Reserve requires existing US Bank banking relationship (checking/savings). | [WEB] DoC |
| **No hard card limit** | No strict max, but limits total credit extension. More existing US Bank cards = harder approval. | [WEB] DoC |

## Bank of America

| Rule | Detail | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| **2/3/4 rule** | Max 2 cards in 2 months, 3 in 12 months, 4 in 24 months. Personal cards only. Business cards excluded. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **24-month rule** | Cannot get card if held it in past 24 months. Enforcement inconsistent. | [WEB] OMAAT |
| **Deposit relationship bonus** | Existing deposit account holders can exceed 2/3/4 limits. Also get 10% rewards bonus. | [WEB] OMAAT |

## Business Card Strategy Note

Business cards are a key tool for staying under 5/24 while earning bonuses:
- **Safe for 5/24**: Chase Ink, Amex Biz, Citi Biz, Barclays Biz, BofA Biz (don't report to personal credit)
- **Counts toward 5/24**: Capital One Biz, Discover Biz, TD Bank Biz (report to personal credit)
- Always verify current reporting behavior before assuming a biz card is "invisible"
