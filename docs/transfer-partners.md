**Strategic implication**: The exclusive partners are what differentiate ecosystems. Choose your primary ecosystem based on which exclusive partner you value most: Hyatt (Chase), Delta (Amex), or AAdvantage (Citi). Universal partners like Flying Blue and KrisFlyer are accessible from any ecosystem.
